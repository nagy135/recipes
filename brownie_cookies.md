# Brownie Cookies

## Ingredients

- 200g chocolate
- 120g butter
- 2ks egg
- 120g sugar
- 2 table spoons of brown sugar
- vanilla extract
- 20g cacao
- 120g flour
- 1/4 tea spoon of baking powder
- chocolate chips

## Instructions

- melt in pot butter, chocolate
- in bowl mix eggs, sugars and whisk it
- add melted mixture
- add rest of ingredients and mix
- bake at 175 degrees 10-12min
