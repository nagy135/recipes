# Doctors Protein Bar

## Ingredients

- Medjool date, roughly chopped - 100g
- Peanut butter, crunchy - 250g
- Protein powder plant based (unflavoured) - 125g
- Almonds (ground) - 100g
- Chia seeds, milled - 75g
- Maple syrup - 2 tbsp
- Water - 175ml
- Dark chocolate (80%), roughly chopped - 150g

## Instructions

1. Line a 20x20cm baking tin with parchment paper.

2. Bring water to boil in a kettle. Pour boiling water over the dates in a bowl - just enough to cover. Soak for 10 minutes, then mash with a potato masher until you have a spreadable paste.

3. Add peanut butter, protein powder, ground almonds, milled chia seeds, maple syrup, and water to a food processor and blend until smooth. Scrape into the prepared tin and press down with the back of a spoon. Spread the mashed dates evenly on top.

4. Place in the fridge for 30 minutes until firm. Slice into 12 even-sized bars.

5. Melt the chocolate in a bowl over a pan of simmering water. Don't let the chocolate touch the base of the pan.

6. Arrange a wire rack over a baking tray. Dip the bars into the chocolate, then place on the wire rack to allow excess to drip off. Sprinkle with toppings of choice while still wet. Allow to set for 5-10 minutes, then store in the fridge.
